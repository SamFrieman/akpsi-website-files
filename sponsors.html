A Message to Our Future Sponsors

At Alpha Kappa Psi's Phi Upsilon Chapter, we prioritize career-readiness and the professional growth of our brothers. Our goal is to the build the next generation of professional leaders and change the way Tampa Bay sees student talent.

The Phi Upsilon Talent Network provides our partners with a direct window into the chapter’s most dedicated student leaders. We’ve curated a private roster of brothers who have consistently demonstrated technical proficiency and a commitment to principled business leadership.

By entering this portal, you skip the traditional search process and gain immediate access to verified resumes and high-GPA candidates tailored to your specific industry needs. We invite you to connect with the next generation of professionals.
