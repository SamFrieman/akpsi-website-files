Life as a Brother

October 5, 1904 - NEW YORK

Our Global Origins

Alpha Kappa Psi traces its prestigious heritage to New York University, where it was founded on October 5, 1904, as the first professional business fraternity in the United States.

April 20, 2024 - TAMPA

Phi Upsilon Legacy

Official campus recognition followed on February 1, 2024. Founding members and early classes have since transformed Phi Upsilon into one of the most elite professional organizations on campus.
